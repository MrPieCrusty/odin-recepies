This is a recipe for a tasty lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for the worlds best lasagna. Lasagna is an Italian dish that is based on pasta plates, tomatoes meat, and cheese. However, this dish can be customized to fit almost everyone, including pesky vegans and gluten intolerant people.

This is a truly amazing dish

Ingredients for 6 people

  • 1 Package of pasta plates
  • 1 Can of crushed tomatoes
  • 800g of minced meat
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 300g butter
  • 400g freshly grated cheese
  • 1 whole onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Steps

  1. Put the oven to 225Â°C. Dice and add the onion to the pan with a drizzle of olive olive
  2. When onion is looking a bit glazed you can add the garlic and letting it fry for 1-2 minutes before adding the meat
  3. While the meat is cooking, melt the butter in a separate pan and add the flour to the melted butter
  4. Add the tomatoes to the meat and onion mix as you add milk slowly to the sauce pan.
  5. When the sauce has reached a good thickness it is time to assemble the lasagna. Add pasta plates, bechamel sauce, and meat sauce until you have completed 3 layers. Top with grated cheese and cook in the oven for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Let the lasagna rest for 5 minutes before serving with some basil and a nice glass of red wine!

Hope you enjoy!!
